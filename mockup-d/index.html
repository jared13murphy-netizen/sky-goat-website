Built for window seats.

Five things Sky Goat does the moment you sit down.

📷

Scan your boarding pass

One PDF417 scan and Sky Goat locks onto your exact flight. Live position, filed route, gate, terminal.

📍

Real-time call-outs

Every couple minutes, Sky Goat tells you what's just to your left or right. Look down.

🌎

Hybrid satellite map

Watch your plane move in real time over a satellite + road overlay map.

📬

Share a postcard

Auto-generated satellite-imagery postcards from your flight. Drop one in iMessage.

🌾

Land for sale below

Live Ground Goat listings within 100 miles of your flight path. Cross-app discovery.

🎯

Flying near

The town directly below you, always visible in the header. So you always know where you are.

When you arrive, Sky Goat auto-generates a satellite-imagery postcard from your actual flight path. Real route, real cities, real stats. Drop it in iMessage and brag a little.
