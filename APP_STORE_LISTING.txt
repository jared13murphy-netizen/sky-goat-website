4. DESCRIPTION  (4000 chars allowed; current draft ~1,946)
================================================================

>>> START — paste into "Description" >>>
The sky just got more interesting.

Sky Goat is your in-flight companion — an iPhone app that turns every window seat into a guided tour of everything you're flying over. Scan your boarding pass, sit back, and watch the goat go to work.

THE GOAT FEED
Every few minutes Sky Goat surfaces something cool below your plane:
• Towns just to your left or right ("Quincy, IL — 13 miles")
• Famous birthplaces ("Bing Crosby was born here")
• Landmarks ("The Gateway Arch is just out the right window")
• Local sports ("Cardinals beat the Reds 5-3 last night")
• Trivia, weather, and what to expect at your destination

LIVE FLIGHT TRACKING
Real ADS-B position, real filed flight plan, real ETA. Watch your plane move on a hybrid satellite + roads map. See your altitude, ground speed, and progress at a glance.

SCAN YOUR BOARDING PASS
One PDF417 scan and Sky Goat locks onto your exact flight. No typing flight numbers. No signing in. Just point and go.

POSTCARDS FROM THE SKY
When you land, Sky Goat auto-generates a satellite-imagery postcard from your actual flight path — real route, real cities, real stats. Drop it in iMessage and brag a little.

GROUND GOAT INTEGRATION
Sky Goat surfaces active Ground Goat land listings within 100 miles of your flight path. Tap to see active auctions and private treaty listings the moment you're flying over them.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT
• Free. Works offline once loaded. No account required.
• 11,570 points of interest, 27,015 town tidbits, 8,001 Wikipedia-verified famous-people birthplaces
• Built-in safety filter that NEVER surfaces flight-disaster content
• Designed for the window seat — every detail tuned for "look down at what?"
• Made by the same team behind Ground Goat (the land-discovery app)

REQUIRES
• iOS 16+
• iPhone with cellular data or in-flight Wi-Fi
• Optional: camera access (boarding pass scanning), location access (precise overhead position)

PRIVACY
Sky Goat works without an account. Your flight history stays on your device unless you tell us otherwise. Read our full privacy policy at sky-goat.com/privacy.

GET IN TOUCH
hello@sky-goat.com
