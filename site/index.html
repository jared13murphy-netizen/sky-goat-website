Built for window seats.

Open Sky Goat when you board, and your goat does the rest.

LOOK OUT THE WINDOW

Real-time call-outs

Every few minutes, Sky Goat tells you what's just to your left or right — rivers, towns, mountains, famous birthplaces.

BOARDING PASS

Scan it once. Done.

One PDF417 scan and Sky Goat locks onto your flight. Live position, filed route, ETA, gate, baggage — all in your pocket.

FLIGHT DETAILS

Everything about your flight

Tap your goat on the map for the full picture: arrival ETA + countdown, gate, terminal, baggage info, altitude, speed, heading, distance remaining, delays.

DISCOVER WHAT'S BELOW

11,000+ points of interest

From Wikipedia POIs to Ground Goat land listings, Sky Goat surfaces the cool things you're literally flying over.

POSTCARDS

One-tap brag.

Auto-generated satellite-imagery postcards from your flight. Real route, real cities, real stats. Drop one in iMessage.

POST FROM THE FLIGHT

Capture the moment

Verified flyers can post photos directly from their flight — pinned to your real altitude and route. Build a flight journal you actually want to revisit. (Coming soon)
