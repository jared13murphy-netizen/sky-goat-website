Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Sky Goat ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Sky Goat does not require an account to use the app. The only personal information we collect is what you actively provide when contacting us:

  • Email address (when you write to support or fill out our contact form)
  • Name (when you provide it in a support inquiry)
  • Any other information you choose to include in your message

Flight Information

When you use Sky Goat to track a flight, the app processes:

  • Flight numbers you enter or scan from a boarding pass
  • Boarding pass barcode data (decoded on-device only; we never receive the raw image)
  • Routes you've watched and miles flown (stored locally on your device, not on our servers)
  • Points of interest viewed during your flight (stored locally for de-duplication)

Device Identifiers

If you allow tracking via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve more relevant ads. If you decline tracking, ads are still displayed but are non-personalized. You can change this preference at any time in your iOS device's Settings.

Location Information

Sky Goat does not currently track your device's location. The position shown on the map is the airplane's position obtained from public flight-tracking APIs, not your phone's GPS. Future updates may request your location for features such as "Flights Near Me," and you will be asked for permission at that time. You can disable location services at any time through your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Sky Goat app and its in-flight features
  • Surface relevant landmarks, towns, points of interest, and weather along your flight path
  • Display advertisements to support the free version of the app
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve the accuracy of our points-of-interest data over time
  • Send important updates about the service (when you contact us)

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Google AdMob, for advertising delivery and measurement
  • Flight-data providers (FlightAware AeroAPI) when you actively track a flight — they receive the flight number and your device's IP address
  • Third-party content providers (ESPN, the National Weather Service, ArcGIS, Ground Goat) when you use features that fetch their data — they receive your device's IP address
  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Advertising

Sky Goat displays in-app advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use information including device identifiers, ad interaction data, and general usage data to deliver ads. Sky Goat does not share your personal information with advertisers. For more information about Google's practices, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Because Sky Goat does not currently store personal information on our servers, your flight history and preferences remain on your device under iOS's built-in protections. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

Your flight history and preferences are stored locally on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the app. Any messages you send us via email or our contact form are retained as long as needed to provide support and resolve your inquiry. You may request deletion of any correspondence at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via iOS's App Tracking Transparency settings
  • Delete all locally stored Sky Goat data by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

Sky Goat integrates with third-party services including FlightAware AeroAPI (flight data), Google AdMob (advertising), ESPN (sports scores), the National Weather Service (weather), ArcGIS (satellite imagery), and Ground Goat (land listings). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

Sky Goat is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us so that we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Sky Goat app after a change indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@groundgoat.com
