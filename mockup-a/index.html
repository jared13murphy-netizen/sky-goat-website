Built for window seats.

Open Sky Goat when you board, and your goat does the rest.

Look out the window

Real-time call-outs tell you what's just to your left or right — rivers, towns, mountains, famous birthplaces.

One scan and Sky Goat locks onto your flight. Live position, filed route, ETA, gate, baggage — all in your pocket.

From Wikipedia POIs to Ground Goat land listings, Sky Goat surfaces the cool things you're literally flying over.

Share a postcard

Auto-generated satellite-imagery postcards from your flight. Drop one in iMessage and brag a little.
